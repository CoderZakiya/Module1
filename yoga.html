🧘 Yoga and Its Benefits

What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient practice that helps improve physical health, mental focus, and inner peace. It combines breathing, stretching, and meditation.

Benefits

  • Improves flexibility
  • Reduces stress
  • Increases strength
  • Improves concentration
  • Boosts overall health

Mental Benefits

Yoga helps reduce anxiety and improves focus and calmness.

Physical Benefits

It strengthens muscles, improves posture, and increases balance. Regular practice keeps the body healthy and active.
