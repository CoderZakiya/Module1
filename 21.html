☀️summer

Summer is the hottest season of the year. It usually comes after spring and before the rainy season. During summer the days are long and the sun shines very brightly. People often feel very hot and drink a lot of water to stay cool. Many fruits such as mangoes, jackfruits, and watermelons grow in this season. Children enjoy their summer holidays and sometimes go to visit relatives or travel to different places. Although summer is very hot, it is also a joyful season for many people.

🌻sunflower

A sunflower is a tall and beautiful plant with a large yellow flower. It is called sunflower because its flower looks like the sun and it often turns toward the sunlight. Sunflowers grow best in warm and sunny places. The seeds of sunflower are very useful and are used to make sunflower oil and food. Farmers grow sunflowers in many countries. The bright yellow petals and big round center make the sunflower one of the most attractive flowers in the world.
