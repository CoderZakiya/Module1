Olympics

The Olympic Games are the world’s biggest and most famous sporting event, bringing together athletes from different countries to compete in various sports every four years. They began in ancient Greece nearly 3,000 years ago and were revived in 1896, becoming a symbol of unity, peace, and sportsmanship. Encyclopedia

"The most important thing in the Olympic Games is not to win but to take part,”

Pierre de Coubertin
This highlights the true spirit of participation. Athletes compete for gold, silver, and bronze medals, showing their talent and hard work.

"Gold medals aren’t really made of gold… they’re made of sweat, determination, and guts,”

Dan Gable
It remindinds us of the effort behind success. The Olympics inspire people around the world by encouraging fitness, dedication, and fair play, making them not just a competition, but a celebration of human spirit and friendship.
