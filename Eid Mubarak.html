Eid Celebration

🌙 Eid Mubarak!
Eid Menu: Prayer | Family | Food | Charity
Eid is one of the most important festivals for Muslims around the world. People celebrate it with prayers, delicious food, and spending time with family.
Before Eid, Muslims prepare their homes and buy new clothes. This box is moved slightly to the right using relative positioning.
🎉 Giving charity and helping the poor is an important part of Eid.










On Eid morning, Muslims gather at mosques or open fields to perform the Eid prayer. After the prayer, people greet each other by saying "Eid Mubarak". Families visit relatives, share meals, and enjoy the joyful atmosphere.
